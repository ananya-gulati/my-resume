About Me

Bridging High-Tech Demands with Time-Tested Mindfulness

I am an Art of Living Teacher, Meditation Trainer, and Principal Software Engineer based in Navi Mumbai. Having spent over 6 years operating in fast-paced, high-pressure software engineering environments, I understand firsthand the realities of modern workplaces—constant connectivity, strict deadlines, shifting technical architectures, and high-performance expectations.

My journey has proven that personal well-being is not a distraction from technical excellence; rather, it is the exact fuel that sustains it. By integrating simple, science-backed breathing practices and meditation into daily routines, we can quiet corporate anxiety and achieve an unshakeable state of flow.

My wellness sessions focus on highly practical, experiential, and interactive breathwork and mindfulness techniques that tech professionals, executives, and individuals can seamlessly fold into their everyday routines.
